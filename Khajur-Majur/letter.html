My Love,

There are thousands of ships sailing across oceans right now. But only one of them carries the person who means the most to me.

Sometimes I miss you so much that I find myself staring at your photos and videos. Sometimes I replay your voice notes in my mind because hearing your voice makes everything feel okay.

I love your childish side. I love your jealous side. I love every version of you.

Thank you for entering my life and turning ordinary days into something I look forward to.

The first thing I want to do when we finally meet is hug you tightly and make up for every mile this long-distance journey placed between us.

And I promise you something...

No matter how many years this distance lasts... No matter how many oceans separate us... I will continue choosing you. Again. And again. And again. Forever. ❤️

Your Khajur

Final Message

No matter how many oceans you cross, my heart will always wait for you on the shore. ⚓❤️
